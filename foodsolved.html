There is tremendous dietary range among turtles.

Turtles

They are omnivorous. Some turtles eat jellyfish and salps.

Tortoises

They are a type of turtle, are predominantly herbivores,but some will consume invertebrates and carrion.

Other

turtles consume waterside vegetation. Some turtles even crush shells of mollusks in order to obtain the meat.
